Welcome message from the Proprietor

Prosperious school was established in 1998 by Mr Omolewu Sunday with the main goal of providing primary education through a stimulating curriculum with help from a very devoted and nururing staff.

our goals are accomplished through the efforts of a professioal, caring and dedicated staff that inspire childeren, involve parents and support colleagues, so that all children successfully achieve a love of learning, confidence in themselves and respect for others. We always try to make our preschool an optimum environment for all children.
